Cooperative Challenge

In this challenge, all team members will work together as a team to accomplish individual and team goals. For example, if there are 5 team members, and each team member is given a goal of 5 classifications each within a determined period, when each team member accomplishes their individual goal, they will also accomplish an overall team goal of 25 classifications.

Competitive Challenge

In this challenge team members will compete against one another to complete the most classifications within a given time frame. The status of the challenge will be shown as a ranked listing including a classification count for each user.

Team Vs. Team

In this challenge, teams will compete against one another to complete the most classifications within a given time frame. The score of each team will be a sum of all classifications completed by each team.
